The Seven Liberal Arts

I. Grammar

The Art of the correct usage of words according to the conventions of language

IV. Arithmetic

The Art of Number, or quanitiy and its symbolic operations

II. Logic

The Art of discerning truth from falsehood by use of dialectic and valid reasoning

V. Geometry

The Art of Number in Space, and the noblest and most ancient Art

III. Rhetoric

The Art of right and persuasive speech by use of the two prior Arts, as well the principles of Pathos, Ethos, and Logos

VI. Music

The Art of Number in Time, namely in patterns and harmonies of sound

VII. Astronomy

The Art of Number in Space and Time, namely in the motions of the heavenly bodies
